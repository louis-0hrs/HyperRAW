HyperRAW is a high-performance RAW video converter designed to process Blackmagic RAW (BRAW) footage into common, editable formats.

The tool focuses on fast, reliable batch conversion and efficient processing pipelines, making it suitable for post-production workflows, archival tasks, and automated media processing.

HyperRAW operates as a BRAW-to-X converter, enabling users to transform BRAW files into formats compatible with standard video and image pipelines, without requiring a full editing environment.
